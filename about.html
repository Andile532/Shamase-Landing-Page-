About Us.
A few words about us..

Shamase Transportation is a passenger oriented transport service company based in South Africa. We offer safe, accessible and unique transport service around Gauteng and South Africa in general. We are highly driven by youth engagement and empowerement in the transport sector with the goal of bringing new ideas and lower the cost of private passenger transportation.

We offer safe modern private transportation to schools, workers, individuals and churches with an intention of moving people to their desired locations and ensure that they return back safely. We show care and excellence in private transport service delivery. We highly undertand that one size does not fit all hence, we've designed our portfolio of vehicles to accommodate any group of individuals and trip.
